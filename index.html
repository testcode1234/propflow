property managers

Nine powerful modules, zero complexity. Replace your spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected tools with one platform.

🏠
Portfolio Dashboard
Real-time overview of all properties, occupancy rates, revenue, and maintenance — at a glance, with drill-down analytics.
💰
Automated Rent Collection
ACH, card, and bank transfer support. Automatic reminders, late fees, and instant ledger posting. Zero manual entry.
📝
Leasing Pipeline
Track leads from inquiry to signed lease. AI-powered tenant screening, background checks, and digital signatures built in.
